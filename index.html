Capabilities

01 / 07
01 — Policy

Deterministic policy enforcement

Allow/deny rules per server, per tool, with glob matching. First-match wins. No match means deny. No ambiguity, no exceptions.

02 — Identity

Agent identity and RBAC scopes

Every agent gets its own API key and scoped permissions. Reader, writer, admin, executor. Enforce least-privilege on every single tool call.

03 — Risk

Real-time risk scoring

Every call scored 0–100, weighted on action type, target sensitivity, and behavioral anomaly. Configurable thresholds for flag and deny.

04 — Audit

Cryptographic audit chain

Ed25519 signatures on every entry. SHA-256 hash chain links them together. Tamper-evident by construction. Export the log — anyone can verify independently.

05 — Approval

Human-in-the-loop approvals

High-risk calls pause and wait for human review. Approve or deny from the dashboard or CLI. Decisions are cryptographically signed into the audit trail.

06 — Gateway

MCP gateway with OAuth broker

One entry point for all your MCP servers. Quint multiplexes stdio and HTTP backends, handles OAuth for GitHub, Notion, and Slack, and injects tokens from an encrypted vault. Agents never see upstream credentials.

07 — Local

Everything stays on your machine

Single Go binary. No cloud. No account. No telemetry. The agent doesn’t even know Quint is there. Your data is yours.
